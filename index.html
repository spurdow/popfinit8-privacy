Last updated: November 1, 2025

However, third-party ad networks (for example, Google AdMob) may collect limited information automatically, such as device identifiers, IP address, and usage data, to serve relevant ads and prevent fraud.

2. How Information Is Used

  • To analyze app usage and improve user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service providers that may collect information as described above:

4. Children’s Privacy

Popfinit8 is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
